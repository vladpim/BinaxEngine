================================================================================
THIRD-PARTY LIBRARIES
================================================================================

Below are the main libraries used in the project.
Full license texts are available in the file ThirdPartyLicenses.txt.

- ASSIMP (Open Asset Import Library) – 3D model loading.
  Authors: Kim Kulling, Alexander Gessler, and others.
  License: BSD 3-Clause.

- BULLET PHYSICS – physics engine.
  Author: Erwin Coumans and others.
  License: zlib.

- GLFW – window creation and input handling.
  Authors: Marcus Geelnard, Camilla Löwy and others.
  License: zlib/libpng.

- GLEW – OpenGL extensions.
  Authors: Milan Ikits, Marcelo Magallon and others.
  License: BSD 3-Clause / MIT.

- GLM (OpenGL Mathematics) – mathematics library.
  Author: Christophe Riccio (g-truc).
  License: MIT.

- IMGUI (Dear ImGui) – editor graphical user interface.
  Author: Omar Cornut.
  License: MIT.

- IMGUIZMO (ImGuizmo) – transformation gizmos in the editor.
  Author: Cedric Guillemet.
  License: MIT.

- LUA – scripting language.
  Author: Roberto Ierusalimschy and others.
  License: MIT.

- MINIAUDIO – audio engine.
  Author: David Reid.
  License: MIT / Public Domain.

- NLOHMANN/JSON – JSON handling.
  Author: Niels Lohmann.
  License: MIT.

- SOL2 – Lua and C++ integration.
  Author: Nathan Reed (ThePhD).
  License: MIT.

- STB (stb_image.h) – texture loading.
  Author: Sean Barrett.
  License: Public Domain / MIT.
